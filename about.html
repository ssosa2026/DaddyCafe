Daddy's Cafe is a cozy cafe filled with love, affection, warmth and the smell of coffee. Currently found in every corner of our round world, across all 8 continents, and 196 countries.All branches are facilitated and maintained by single dads all around the globe, and customers may come in at any day, at any point of time. The dads will grind the beans, brew the powder, and server the coffee to you, they may even provide you certain special treatments, for whenever you feel lonely and want to dine with someone, the dads can pretend to be your friend, your lover, or your actual dad! Though note that these may come at a more heftier price than the food itself.

Daddy's Cafe also has multiple dining options, you may opt for a cozy dining experience, a quick and easy take-out, or maybe even a speedy and convenient delivery. All of these options are available to choose from whenever you order from any branch of Daddy's Cafe.

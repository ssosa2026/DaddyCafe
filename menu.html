Looking for a place to get a drink? Want some time to yourself and unwind to some great coffee? Come visit us at Daddy's Cafe, now coming to places near You! Yes, You!

Daddy's Coffee is a great place to get a drink with a childhood friend, by yourself, or, as the name suggests, with your dad! Daddy's Cafe serves coffees ranging from Lattes, to Espressos, to literally just pure caffeine in a mug.(If you'd like it.)



-Drinks-

  •    "I Can't Espress my feelings for you."(Espresso) - Intense and Flavorful. No other additives.
    Available sizes:   Small(80 pesos),   Medium(105 pesos),   Large(120 pesos)

  •    Cappucino - One part espresso, One part textured milk, One part froth on top, usually dusted with chocolate. Avaliable Sizes:   Small(89 pesos),   Medium(113 pesos),   Large(126 pesos)

  •    Macchiato - A part of espresso followed by steamed milk, finished with a small layer of foam.
    Available Sizes:   Small(91 pesos),   Medium(113 pesos),   Large(126 pesos)

  •    "Missing you a Latte."(Latte) - Structurally similar to a cappucino, but instead uses steamed milk instead of textured milk, and has foamed milk crema on top.
    Available Sizes:   Small(92 pesos),   Medium(115 pesos),   Large(130 pesos)

  •    Mocha - A Latte with added chocolate.
    Available Sizes:   Small(94 pesos),   Medium(116 pesos),   Large(130 pesos)
